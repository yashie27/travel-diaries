Oh Sweet Venice

I honestly feel like my time in Venice was something I will remember forever. It was so beautiful; I've never been to a more serene city. My favorite part was definitely the Grand Canal. I hope I can return again one summer.

What Happens In Vegas...

Going to Vegas on my 21st birthday was definitely a night to remember. It was lively, colorful, and bustling with people and sounds. Not sure if I'd ever wanna go back, but I'm glad I got to experience it at least once in my life.

Poconos Disaster

Went on a skiing trip this December with a group of my friends and it was... a nightmare to say the least. I've never done skiing before, but I didn't think it would be THAT difficult. I fell at least sixty-three times that day.
